Improve your fitness and Use Public Transport

Petrol and diesel cars release a lot of climate-wrecking emissions. They also increase air pollution.

Improve your fitness and tackle climate change at the same time by cycling or walking. Too tired? Hop on the bus or train.

Get your electricity from the wind and the sun.

When you switch to a green-energy provider, you will be helping the movement for more renewable sources.

You could even be in for a surprise saving. Some green-energy providers to look at include but are not limited to:

  • British gas
  • E.ON
  • SSC

Eat Less Meat

We are not telling you to "go Vegan", but eating less meat will help to reduce the amount of emissions being emitted.

Meat and dairy production is responsible for 14.% of global climate-changing emissions. Eating too much meat is not good for your health either. Each year we could prevent thousands of early deaths in the UK, and save the NHS a fortune, by changing to low-meat or no-meat diets.
